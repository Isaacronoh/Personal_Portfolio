My CV


My name is Isaac Ronoh, a passionate and highly motivated ICT professional based in Kenya, with a strong foundation in Information Communication Technology and Technical Trainer Education. I specialize in technical training, computer support and maintenance, networking, and web development, with additional experience in digital literacy, data systems, and environmental conservation initiatives.

Over the years, I have built a diverse skill set through both academic training and hands-on experience. I hold a Diploma in ICT and a Diploma in Technical Trainer Education, which have equipped me with the expertise to train learners effectively, manage ICT resources, and implement technology-driven solutions in educational and organizational settings.



My professional journey has included roles such as:

  • ICT Trainer and Computer Lab Technician at Wajir East TVC
  • Teacher on Practice at Samburu TVC
  • Computer Instructor and Lab Assistant at Chemaner VTC
  • GTAP Coordinator for Bomet County
  • Environmental Conserver with Green Chepalungu CBO







These roles have allowed me to gain valuable experience in curriculum delivery, system administration, hardware and software troubleshooting, and community engagement. I am also actively involved in digital projects, including developing dynamic web applications using Django, supporting online course applications, and integrating email-based communication for educational institutions.

What sets me apart is my ability to adapt to diverse working environments, my strong sense of responsibility, and my passion for empowering others through technology. I am driven by continuous learning, teamwork, and innovation, and I am always open to opportunities that allow me to grow professionally while making a meaningful impact.

Whether it's in the classroom, behind a computer, or in the field, I strive to deliver excellence, build solutions that matter, and contribute positively to every team I join.
